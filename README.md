## Why this project exists

Most prompt engineering resources focus on **what to type**.

This handbook focuses on **how to think**.

Prompt Engineering is not simply writing instructions for an AI model. It is the engineering discipline of designing reliable communication between humans and AI systems.

The goal of this project is to help you build practical skills that remain valuable as AI models continue to evolve.

## Engineering Philosophy

PromptForge follows one simple principle:

> Build AI systems that are understandable, reliable, and reusable.

Every chapter is built around three questions:

1. What is the concept?
2. Why does it matter?
3. How can you apply it?

Every important concept should include:

- explanation;
- examples;
- best practices;
- common mistakes;
- exercises or labs.
